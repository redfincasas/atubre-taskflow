🚨 Critical Updates
HIGH PRIORITY · CONFIRMED ACTIVE

1. Nigeria Raw Shea Export Ban — Confirmed Active Through February 25, 2027

Nigeria's raw shea nut export ban, originally announced August 2025, was formally extended February 26, 2026 by President Tinubu for one additional year through February 25, 2027. All shea exports must route through the Nigerian Commodity Exchange (NCX); direct shipment exemptions have been removed. Nigeria represents ~40% of global shea supply. Critically: Mali, Côte d'Ivoire, and Togo have adopted similar export restrictions, compounding the West African supply crisis. Nut prices have fallen ~33% domestically (a short-term negative for farmers, but confirms supply is not reaching EU buyers).

Source: The Times Nigeria, Feb 26, 2026 · allAfrica.com, Mar 2, 2026 · HPC Mag MEA, Mar 4, 2026 · BeautyMatter

HIGH PRIORITY · CONFIRMED ACTIVE

2. Burkina Faso — Indefinite Export Suspension Confirmed, No End Date

Burkina Faso's full shea nut export suspension is confirmed. An initial December 2025–May 2026 export window was abandoned when authorities found local processors still unable to secure raw materials despite priority access rules. Smuggling operations (1,800+ tonnes seized in one enforcement operation) further complicated supply. The indefinite suspension remains in force with no announced end date. Combined with Nigeria's ban and similar restrictions in Mali, Côte d'Ivoire, and Togo: this is an unprecedented multi-country West African supply disruption with no near-term resolution visible.

Source: Tridge · Ecofin Agency · Premium Beauty News · Pravda Burkina Faso, Nov 2025

REGULATORY · 22 DAYS

4. EU Omnibus VIII (Regulation 2026/78) — Applies May 1, 2026

Regulation (EU) 2026/78 bans 15 new CMR (carcinogenic, mutagenic, reprotoxic) substances from EU cosmetics. Shea butter is fully unaffected and compliant. Deadline: May 1, 2026 — 22 days from now. Brands reformulating away from restricted substances will increase demand for clean, compliant emollients. Additionally, 56 new fragrance allergens require revised labeling by July 31, 2026 — brands with affected products are actively reformulating, opening further demand for non-allergenic natural emollients like Nilotica.

Source: Cosmeservice · EU Verify · Personal Care Insights · Cosmetics & Toiletries

REGULATORY · ACTIVE

5. EU Regulation 2025/877 (CMR) — In Force Since September 1, 2025. Compliance Deadline July 31, 2026.

Products placed on EU market must comply with CMR restrictions by July 31, 2026. Non-compliant products already on market may continue until July 31, 2028. Shea butter is not a CMR substance. REACH: pure, unmodified shea butter is exempt from registration regardless of import volume. Chemically modified products require registration if >1 tonne/year.

Source: CBI Market Entry Report, January 7, 2026 · REACH24H
